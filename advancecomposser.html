Why Compress Images?

Image compression is essential for faster website loading, reduced bandwidth usage, and improved user experience. Our tool helps you find the perfect balance between quality and file size.

Tips for Best Results

  • For photographs, use JPG or WEBP format with quality around 70-80%
  • For images with transparency, use PNG or WEBP
  • WEBP typically offers the best compression with good quality
  • Try different quality settings to find the sweet spot for your needs
